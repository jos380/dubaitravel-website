Must‑Visit Destinations

  • Burj Khalifa: World's tallest building and stunning observation decks.
  • Dubai Marina: Luxury waterfront with skyscrapers and yacht cruises.
  • Dubai Mall: Premier shopping and entertainment destination.
  • Desert Safari: Dune bashing, camel rides, and Bedouin‑style camps.
  • Dubai Museum & Al Fahidi Fort: Window to Dubai’s history and culture.
